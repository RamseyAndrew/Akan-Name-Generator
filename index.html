AkanNameGenerator

The Akan Name Generator is a web application designed to determine and display a user’s Akan name based on the day of the week they were born and their gender. Akan names are traditional Ghanaian names assigned to the day of birth and differ for males and females. The application collects the user’s date of birth (day, month, and year) and gender through a form. Using a mathematical formula, the app calculates the exact day of the week the user was born. Based on the result and the selected gender, the app maps the day to a corresponding Akan name from predefined arrays of male and female names. The chosen Akan name, along with the day of the week, is then dynamically displayed to the user in a user-friendly message. This project not only offers a cultural insight into Akan traditions but also provides a fun, interactive way for users to learn about the significance of their birth day in a different cultural context.
